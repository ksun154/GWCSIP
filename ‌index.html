Top 4 Places I Have Traveled To

Gothic Quarter- Barcelona, Spain

The Gothic Quarter in Barcelona is a beautiful, winding area filled with history, good food, shops, and the best gelato.

Mount Rigi- Luzern, Switzerland

Mount Rigi's majestic peninsula provides great hiking trails for an authentic Swiss mountain experience with unforgettable views of over 13 lakes and the spectacular snow-capped Swiss Alps.

Tracy Arm Fjord- Alaska, USA

Cruise to Tracy Arm Fjord, Alaska and awe at a landscape carved by glaciers with hundreds of waterfalls cascading down 3000-foot granite walls.

Mount Emei- Sichuan, China

Mount Emei is one of the Four Sacred Buddhist Mountains of China. This is the location of the first Buddhist temple as well as seventy-six Buddhist monasteries of the Ming and Qing period, most of them located near the mountain top.
